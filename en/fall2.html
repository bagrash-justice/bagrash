What Happened

After the verdict of 22.07.2024 Bagrash filed an appeal. Defence counsel RA Römer submitted the grounds of appeal to the BGH on 28.11.2024. Bagrash had no contact with his counsel and did not know what he had submitted.

Bagrash therefore submitted 8 supplementary grounds of his own between January and May 2025. Their acceptance was systematically blocked by detention facility staff and court clerks — on direct instructions from management.

Key violation: The BGH on 07.05.2025 dismissed the appeal by a formulaic ruling — without substantively examining a single legal argument. The BGH did not respond to any urgent requests and issued no confirmations of receipt (§ 37 StPO).

Selected Statements from Sworn Affidavits

15.01.2025: The court clerk refused to accept the supplementary grounds — "on direct instructions from Ms Lukas and VRiLG Groß". Statement: "We will under no circumstances accept your complaint, whatever grounds you put forward."
24.02.2025: Double refusal after reading the content — immediate complaint and appeal. The detention facility officer present stated: "There is no point writing so much." He was clearly aware of the contents of documents subject to official secrecy.
18.03.2025: Triple refusal — supplementary grounds + criminal complaint against VRiLG Groß + complaint of violation of the right to be heard — all rejected simultaneously.

Hunger Strike from 05.05.2025

On 5 May 2025 Bagrash announced a hunger strike — as a last resort against the institutional blockade of his fundamental rights. The statement was sent to more than 40 recipients — including Federal President Steinmeier, members of the Bundestag and international human rights organisations.
