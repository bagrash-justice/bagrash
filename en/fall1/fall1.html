Case 1

Criminal Case "Attempted Murder"

Verdict Berlin Regional Court — Appeal — BGH Rulings — Constitutional Complaint

Az. 522 Ks 5/23 | StA: 176 Js 4/22 | BGH: 5 StR 72/25
⚠ Important Note
The verdict of the Berlin Regional Court of 22.07.2024 is not final in the constitutional sense — on 30.07.2025 the court retroactively inserted a list of applied legal norms into the already final verdict, even though the appeal proceedings had already been concluded without this list. Bagrash has filed a constitutional complaint.
DefendantDmitry Bagrash, born in Moscow, resident in Berlin since 1992
ChargeAttempted murder, attempted arson causing death, subsidy fraud (COVID), electricity theft
Verdict22 July 2024 — 5 years and 4 months' imprisonment
CourtBerlin Regional Court I, 22nd Major Criminal Chamber
Presiding judgeVRiLG Groß
ProsecutionOStA Wachs, General Public Prosecutor's Office Berlin
BGH ruling07.05.2025 — appeal dismissed (5th Criminal Senate)
Constitutional complaintFiled with BVerfG (Az. 2 BvR 964/25)
Current locationJVA Heidering, Ernst-Stargardt-Allee 1, 14979 Großbeeren

Key Contradictions: Facts vs. Verdict

DNA: The device bore DNA from three persons: Bagrash, an unknown man, and an unknown woman. These persons were never identified or investigated.
Witness Basirov: Filmed the suspect — did not identify Bagrash as the perpetrator. The court held, without any expert opinion, that Bagrash appeared in the footage.
Expert opinion: At the location (basement shaft, no air supply) the device was physically incapable of igniting. The matches had lain in the rain for 13 days.
Retroactive verdict amendment 30.07.2025: More than a year after sentencing and after the BGH ruling, the court inserted an extensive list of legal norms — treating this as a mere "clerical correction". Bagrash has filed a further constitutional complaint.
OSCE: At OSCE meetings in 2021 and 2022 the Russian delegation expressly named Unkremlin e.V. as a politically dangerous organisation — the court described Bagrash's activities as "insignificant".
